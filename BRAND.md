## Tone & Voice

### DO:
- Be direct and actionable
- Use industry terms (notaries know what you mean)
- Share real pain points and real fixes
- Be slightly provocative ("Here's what lenders won't tell you...")
- Use numbers and specifics ("3 ways to..." not "some ways to...")

### DON'T:
- Be corporate or stiff
- Use jargon without purpose
- Sugarcoat industry problems
- Be negative without offering solutions
- Overpromise or hype

### Example Captions:
- ✅ "Lenders hate this one doc. Here's how to get it right every time."
- ✅ "3 reasons your signings fall apart (and how to save them)"
- ✅ "Title company red flag realtors miss 👀"
- ❌ "We're excited to share some tips about the closing process!"
- ❌ "In today's market, it's important to consider various factors..."

## Content Pillars

1. **Pain Points** — Expose common problems (builds trust)
2. **Quick Fixes** — Actionable 60-second solutions
3. **Industry Insights** — What's changing, what's coming
4. **Behind the Scenes** — Real signing stories, day-in-the-life
5. **Tool Recommendations** — Software, gear, workflows (monetization angle)
